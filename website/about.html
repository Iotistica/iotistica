About Iotistica

We build production-grade IoT infrastructure for industrial and commercial businesses.

Iotistica is a Toronto-based software company specializing in the infrastructure that powers industrial IoT. We solve the complex challenges of reliable device connectivity, high-throughput data ingestion, and large-scale remote fleet management, enabling organizations to deploy and operate connected systems with confidence.

Our platform is purpose-built for real-world industrial environments where connectivity is unpredictable, sensor data is continuous, and managing thousands of distributed devices must happen remotely. From edge gateways to cloud infrastructure, we deliver end-to-end solutions that are secure, scalable, and engineered for long-term reliability.

We partner with industrial and commercial organizations across North America to build IoT systems tailored to their operational needs. Rather than forcing customers into a one-size-fits-all platform, we design solutions that integrate seamlessly with existing hardware, communication protocols, and data architectures. Whether you're connecting a small network of Modbus PLCs or managing a nationwide fleet streaming millions of telemetry events, Iotistica provides the flexibility, performance, and reliability to support your deployment at every stage.
