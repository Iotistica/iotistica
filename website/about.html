About Iotistica

We build production-grade IoT infrastructure for industrial and commercial businesses.

Iotistica is a software company focused on the hard parts of IoT — reliable device connectivity, high-throughput data ingestion, and remote fleet management at scale. We've built our platform from the ground up to handle the realities of industrial environments: intermittent connectivity, high-frequency sensor data, and the need to manage thousands of devices without on-site intervention.

We don't believe in cookie-cutter products. Every customer has a different hardware stack, different protocols, and different data retention requirements. Our platform adapts to fit — whether you're connecting a handful of Modbus PLCs or streaming telemetry from a nationwide fleet.

Based in Toronto, Canada. We work with industrial and commercial businesses across North America, delivering custom IoT solutions from edge hardware to cloud infrastructure.
